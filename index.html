About Me

I graduated from Metropolitan State University early in 2018. After graduating, I worked as a psyche associate for two years. I currently work as a Behavioral therapist. I work with kids with autism. In my community, parents don't have access to resources to help them when their kids approach a kid when they are exhibiting certain behaviors, and how they can reduce that behavior or anticipate it and deal with said behavior. I have always been interested in coding and finally decided it was time to follow that interest and see where it takes me and create something that will have a real-life impact.

After two years of working as a behavioral therapist, I decided to code, and hopefully, change careers. I like the challenge of solving problems; the idea of creating something is fascinating, working with other developers to create something that can change the lives of people. I hope to create a website or a platform that will give parents access to the resources they need to help their kids deal with the challenges they face. Web development offers a ton of room for creativity, and I love that you are always learning something new. I welcome all the challenges ahead of me, and I hope to gain a lot from this journey. At thinkful, I hope to achieve the right skills and become a developer.
